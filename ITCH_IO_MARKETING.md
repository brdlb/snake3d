**Yes, the controls are weird. It's QEAD.**

Why? Because in a true 3D Snake game, standard controls don't work.
*   If you look down, you see nothing but your own tail.
*   If you look up, your massive body blocks the entire view ahead.

We solved this by removing "Up", "Down", "Left", and "Right" entirely. Instead, you control your **Orientation**. Use **Q** and **E** to roll, **A** and **D** to turn. 

To master this game, you must unlearn the concepts of "up" and "down". There is only *forward* and *relative rotation*. It will break your brain for the first 10 minutes. Then, it will click, and you will understand why this is the only way to play 3D Snake properly.
